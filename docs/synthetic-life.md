- **Contradiction Resolution**: Detects logical friction (e.g., competing facts about the same entity) and triggers rituals to establish truth.
- **Hotspot Detection**: Identifies areas of high activity but low knowledge density, directing cognitive resources where they are most needed.

- **Zombie Pruning**: Removes "dead" data that hasn't been accessed and fails to contribute to reasoning fitness.
- **Self-Experimentation**: Conducts "Ablation Tests" by temporarily disabling knowledge to objectively measure its impact on success rates.
- **Auto-Recovery**: If performance degrades during an experiment, the system automatically "remembers" the disabled knowledge, ensuring survival.

- **Type Regeneration**: Instantly regenerates TypeScript types to match its new physical structure, allowing it to "grow into" its new capabilities.
- **DNA Inversion**: The ability to roll back entire structural evolutions if they fail to meet safety or performance baselining.

- **Trait Sharing**: If one persona discovers a successful role or policy, it is cross-pollinated into other personas via the `HiveLink`.
- **Lesson Injection**: Distills core "wisdom" from successful sessions into the global systemic goals.
